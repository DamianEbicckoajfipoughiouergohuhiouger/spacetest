Welcome to the World of Technology and Space

About Us

Who we are?

We are the Space And Technology Club from Amerikanska Gymnasiet Stockholm. We focus mostly on researching space and building different types of tech.

What we do?

Our club is divided into two departments; the Coding Department which handles the software and code side of things, programming for hardware or creating digital media. We also have the Practical department which handles all hands-on tasks such as building things and working on physical stuff.

Why join us?

Joining our club will give you an opportunity to learn and explore your interest in technology and space. You will have a chance to meet like-minded people and work on exciting projects that will enhance your skills and knowledge.
