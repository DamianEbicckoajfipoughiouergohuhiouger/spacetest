About Us

Who we are?

We are a passionate group of students who love technology and space. Our club is divided into two departments, Coding and Practical.

What we do?

In the Practical department, we work on building robots and rockets for various purposes. In the Coding department, we work on various coding projects including websites and games.

Why join us?

Joining our club will give you an opportunity to learn and explore your interest in technology and space. You will have a chance to meet like-minded people and work on exciting projects that will enhance your skills and knowledge.
